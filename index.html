Sep 19, 2020 - Towards Data Science

Sinapsi is a web application designed to provide an easy connection between italian family doctors and psychologists, dispensing also useful informative content. Sinapsi's services are meant to be used from trusted family doctors only and are therefore password-protected using a clinet-side AES-256 encryption.
